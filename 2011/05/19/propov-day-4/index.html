ProPOV Day 4

19 May, 2011

Today was all about assembly. I finished tinning the boards, got out my components, and soldered away! The board I chose to solder had horrible shorts, mainly because I tinned with too much solder in some places. The traces were a bit close together, too. Next time I will use narrower traces with more free space between them.
