ProPOV Build Screenshots

26 May, 2011

For my capstone presentation I compiled a video of the ProPOV work screenshots. To capture the images I wrote a bash script that uses scrot to save a screenshot every twelve seconds. The video plays the screenshots at two frames per second.

Ed. Note: The screencast video was lost during a site migration.

I also created a poster for the presentation, and my dad and I designed a mounting system of Lexan and brass bolts to hold the spinning clock.
