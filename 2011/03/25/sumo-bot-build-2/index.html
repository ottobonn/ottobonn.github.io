Sumo Bot Build 2

25 March, 2011

The SumoBox is fully assembled, but it weighs too much! It weighs in at 591 g with all eight AA batteries and the luxury of a (heavy) solderless breadboard. To shave off some grams, I think I will bore out the rear axle holder (a repurposed old computer heatsink) and shorten the bumpers and sidewalls as much as possible.
