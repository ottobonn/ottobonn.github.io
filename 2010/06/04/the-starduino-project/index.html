The Starduino Project

4 June, 2010

My first major foray into the wonderful world of Arduino prototyping and programming is the “Starduino.” It’s a paper star lantern filled with LEDs that glows in soothing, natural patterns. I used an RBBB Kit from moderndevice as the main processor, and worked out the circuitry and mounting myself.

You can read through the whole process and make one yourself right here. It was a pleasure to make and very fun to watch when completed. I am releasing the code and schematics in hopes that others will enjoy the Starduino as much as I do.

Happy building!
